OTHER LIFE EVENTS

There are many life events that we want to celebrate. Be it an important anniversary, significant birthday, engagement, retirement or leaving party, divorce, promotion or move of house etc. Working closely together we can design and deliver a ceremony that marks that occasion. Whether it’s a small informal dinner party or a larger formal ceremony there is no better way than to do it with a personal and memorable ceremony.
